Pondicherry University is a prestigious central university established in 1985 by the Government of India. Located in Kalapet along the scenic East Coast Road, the campus spans over 1,000 acres with modern infrastructure, research centers, and serene surroundings.

The university serves multiple Union Territories — Puducherry, Mahe, Yanam, and the Andaman & Nicobar Islands — making it a diverse academic hub. With 14 schools and over 50 departments, it offers programs in science, humanities, engineering, commerce, management, and emerging technologies.

Recognized for its inclusive education policy, Pondicherry University provides free lodging and tuition to differently-abled students, rent-free accommodation for girls, and advanced research opportunities for scholars. Its consistent top rankings reflect the university’s dedication to academic excellence.
