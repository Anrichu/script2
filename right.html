About

The library at Pondicherry University, known as the Ananda Rangapillai Library, stands as a vibrant intellectual hub within the campus, fostering academic excellence and research. Spanning multiple floors, it houses a vast collection of books, journals, e-resources, and rare manuscripts that support a wide array of disciplines. Equipped with digital access terminals, research databases, and a serene reading environment, the library seamlessly blends traditional learning with modern technology. view more books

Its user-friendly services, including remote access to e-resources and a dedicated research wing, make it an essential resource for students, scholars, and faculty, reflecting the university's commitment to knowledge and innovation.

Library Services

Alongside traditional services, the automated library offers:

  • Research assistance and referral services
  • Library Liaison Officers
  • Citation analysis and institutional repository
  • Orientation programmes, workshops, and exhibitions
  • E-alerts, newsfeeds, free trials, open source access
  • Remote login for 24×7 access to all digital resources

The library portal provides access to e-books, e-journals, e-theses, online tutorials, and OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue).

Inclusive access is ensured through the Louis Braille Centre, offering assistive technology for visually challenged users — a feature that earned the university a national award in 2011.
